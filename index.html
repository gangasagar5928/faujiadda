The Process

new home in 4 steps.

Set Your Destination & Rank

Enter your new posting station. Select your rank category (OR, JCO, or Officer) to automatically filter out properties that exceed your authorized BAH limits.

Scan the Live Map

Browse our custom Dark Mode map. See color-coded pins showing exact pricing and availability dates, mapped strictly around the Cantonment parameters.

Check Gate Distances

Forget "distance to city center." Faujiadda calculates the exact driving distance from the property directly to the nearest Military Station gate.
