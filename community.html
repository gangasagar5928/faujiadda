Community Guidelines

Our platform relies on mutual trust. Keep the eco-system clean, transparent, and respectful.

🚫 No Brokers Allowed

Faujiadda is designed to prevent predatory commission structures explicitly targeting transferred defence files. If you are a commercial property broker posing as an owner, your IP and phone number will be blocklisted permanently instantly upon user report.

📸 Genuine Photos Only

Do not upload generic internet stock images. Our AI engine flags stock imagery, but you are required to upload real photos of the interior. Listings defaulting to generic photos are ranked lowest geographically.

💰 Accurate Pricing vs BAH

List exactly what the user must pay per month. Do not artificially lower the listing rent purely to fit inside an `OR (15k)` BAH filter, only to add hidden "maintenance charges" upon phone contact. Transparency is mandatory.

🤝 Honour Agreements

If you verbally confirm a lease hand-off to a transferring fauji family, delete your listing from the App. Leaving an occupied house listed clutters the feed for others desperately seeking housing under quick posting orders.
