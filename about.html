About Faujiadda

Every year, thousands of Indian Armed Forces personnel are transferred to new duty stations. For an Officer, JCO, or OR, the challenge isn’t just packing up a life—it’s finding a safe, affordable, and trustworthy place to stay in an unfamiliar city.

Traditional real estate platforms don't understand the unique constraints of military life. They don't track distances from Cantonment gates, they don't filter listings against authorized BAH (Basic Allowance for Housing) limits, and most importantly, they charge exorbitant one-month brokerage fees to families who are forced to move constantly.

Our Mission

"To build a zero-brokerage, high-trust digital ecosystem exclusively for India's defence community."

Built by the Community, For the Community

Faujiadda was conceptualized to solve the housing gap for military personnel. We mandate universal OTP verifications to filter out scammers. We use advanced Map integrations to show precise driving realities rather than "as-the-crow-flies" guesses. Furthermore, our proprietary AI Fair Price Engine prevents predatory landlords from overcharging incoming defence families by providing instant market analyses.

Supporting Future Warriors

We haven't stopped at serving active-duty personnel. Our platform uniquely integrates an SSB Dorm Finder, indexing safe, verified, and budget-friendly accommodations across all 12 major selection centres in India for the next generation of military officers.
