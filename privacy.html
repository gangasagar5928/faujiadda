Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 28, 2026

At Faujiadda, safeguarding the privacy of our Indian Armed Forces community is our highest priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication Data: Phone numbers (via OTP) or Email addresses used exclusively to verify identity and restrict broker spam.
  • Listing Data: Property details, exact geographic lat/longs, images, and authorized contact info provided by posting owners.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is strictly utilized to operate and improve exactly what Faujiadda offers: a peer-to-peer housing map. This includes filtering properties, determining "Distance to Cantt", and evaluating AI Fair Price estimates. We do not sell your personal data to third-party ad networks or brokers.

3. Data Protection & Obfuscation

Your contact detail (e.g., WhatsApp number) is masked by default on the platform. It is only revealed to authenticated, logged-in users to prevent unverified scraping by external brokers or agencies.

4. Your Rights

You have the full right to edit or delete your listings at any time. If you delete a listing, all associated media and location data is permanently purged from our primary databases.

5. Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Significant modifications will be communicated via platform announcements.
